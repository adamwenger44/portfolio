About me

My name is Adam Wenger. I am 21 years old and am currently taking classes at UNCC for a web developement bootcamp. There is a bit of a backstory to why I am here today. I went to Pitt Community College next to ECU out of high school, and the plan was to transfer to ECU acter I completed my 2 years at Pitt. I enjoyed the freedom of college but I did not have the self dicipline to complete my courses to the best of my ability. So I returned home.



I worked meaningless jobs for about a year until I found out my girlfriend was pregnant. My friend got me a job in construction and it was a decent living, not great, but we were making it. After a year of doing this I realized I wanted more with my life, my back was in pain every day and I wasn't using the brain that I knew I could use.

Once I complete this program I hope to get a good job and be able to provide a good life for my family.
